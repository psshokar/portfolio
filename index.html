About Preetam:

Born and raised in southeast Michigan, I am the youngest of four siblings.

Growing up in the suburbs of Detroit, I enjoyed the fun and challenges that Michigan had to offer.

I made the most of beautiful summers, playing basketball and rollerblading around my neighborhood.

Fall came and with it came bonfires, hay rides, and pumpkin picking with my three older siblings.

Winter brought lots of snow, sledding, and family time around the holidays.

Spring was filled with gardening and getting back outdoors.
